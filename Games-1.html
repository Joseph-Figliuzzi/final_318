Activity Info

Zero-Gravity Laser Tag

Laser tag is a recreational shooting sport where participants use infrared-emitting light guns to tag designated targets. Infrared-sensitive signaling devices are commonly worn by each player to register hits and are sometimes integrated within the arena in which the game is played.


Zero-Gravity Laser Tag however, is played in an arena with artifical zero-gravity. walk on the walls. walk on the ceilings. Float around through the center of the room. No longer is the floor yours, but the whole room is too.

Zero-Gravity obstacle course

Zero-Gravity obstacle course is a series of obstacles, hoops to jump through, and mysterious physical puzzles to navigate as you attempt to fly to the target destination

Zero-Gravity soccer

It is just like soccer, except the arena is a sphere, and you play on the insdie. Pass the ball, pass the ball down, go in which ever directon you wish as you aim to score in the goal on the opposite side.
